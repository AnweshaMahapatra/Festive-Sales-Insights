## 🔍 Two Products That Changed the Conversation

Before dashboards, there was SQL.

Raw queries. Direct answers.

One question stood out:

👉 *Find products above ₹500 under BOGOF.*

The result came back with just two products 👇

- 🌧️ **Atliq Waterproof Immersion Rod ₹1,190**  
- 🛏️ **Atliq Double Bedsheet Set ₹1,020**

BOGOF on ₹1,000+ products 😳

That means:

- Effective price per unit drops to ~₹595  
- 💥 ~50% margin impact absorbed by the business  

A risky bet.

But the data later confirmed something surprising:

✔️ It *worked* — in some cases *extremely well*

- 🏆 Atliq Waterproof Immersion Rod: **266.19% IR%**  
  👉 Highest across both campaigns  

The sacrifice paid off… but only because the data proved it.

## 💣 The Three Promotions That Broke the Assumption

Everyone expects discounts to work.

But the data said otherwise 👇

- ❌ 25% OFF → **–₹3M**
- ❌ 33% OFF → **–₹2M**
- ❌ 50% OFF → **–₹1M**

🚨 All negative impact.

Why?

Because math is ruthless:

- 33% OFF → needs **50% more volume just to break even**
- 50% OFF → needs **100% more volume**

And that didn’t happen.

📌 Result:

👉 More discount ≠ more profit  
👉 More discount = predictable loss
